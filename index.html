I am an Associate Professor of Electrical Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). I received my PhD, supervised by Prof. Wei Wei

, in Electrical Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2023 where I specialized in power system operations, control, and analysis.

My research interests include power system planning and operation with renewable power and energy storage. Specifically, I focus on advanced decision-making methods (optimization, control, learning, etc.) and their applications in energy systems. I have published more than 30 peer-reviewed research papers, including 10 in IEEE Transactions Journals. I am an associate editor of IET Renewable Power Generation and Protection and Control of Modern Power Systems.
